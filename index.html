About the Conference

What is CSNMUN?

Cambridge School Noida Model United Nations is a premier inter-school MUN conference that brings together young minds from across the nation. Our conference provides a platform for students to engage in meaningful discussions on pressing global and national issues, develop public speaking skills, and foster diplomatic thinking.

From the halls of the United Nations General Assembly to the seats of Indian Political Parties, CSNMUN 2026 offers a diverse range of committees designed to challenge, inspire, and transform every delegate. Whether you are a first-timer or a seasoned MUNner, CSNMUN promises an experience like no other.

Open to students of Classes IX through XII, the conference spans two days of rigorous debate, collaboration, and camaraderie — all within the distinguished campus of Cambridge School, Sector 27, Noida.
